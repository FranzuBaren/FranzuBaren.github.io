About

I am a data science professional with a strong quantitative background rooted in theoretical physics and computational neuroscience. My career spans academic research and industry leadership, focusing on the development and deployment of robust AI systems that meet regulatory standards and business objectives.

With expertise in mathematical modeling, statistical inference, and machine learning, I specialize in building data-driven solutions that are both scientifically rigorous and commercially viable. My work emphasizes reproducible research methodologies, ethical AI practices, and the critical intersection of data science with regulatory compliance.

As a data science manager, I lead cross-functional teams in developing AI systems that undergo rigorous audit processes, ensuring they meet the highest standards of reliability, fairness, and regulatory adherence. My approach combines deep technical knowledge with strategic business acumen to deliver impactful, sustainable AI solutions.
