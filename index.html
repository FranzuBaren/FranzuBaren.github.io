About

I studied theoretical physics and then specialized in computational neuroscience before building a dynamic, international career in data science and artificial intelligence across several leading companies. My professional journey is driven by scholarly rigor, translational science, and advancing reproducible, impactful methods in applied AI.

As a former academic researcher now managing data science initiatives in the pharmaceutical sector—with a special focus on analytics within Audit & Risk at Roche— I strive to bridge the gap between cutting-edge innovations and real-world, trustworthy applications in highly regulated environments. I value robust methodology, open science, and transparent collaboration across disciplines.
